EasyCHEF!

EasyCHEF! is a simple web app that supplies recipes for everyone to use! Once your ingredients are entered into the search engine, EasyCHEF! will find a recipe containing the same ingredients. This allows you to try something different, or to be adventurous when testing your skills in the kitchen! This web app is designed to promote healthy eating, and an alternative choice for your meals.

An easy choice of healthy eating foods!

Are you tired of eating the same food? Do you feel like trying something new or simply test your culinary skills? Then EasyCHEF! is the right app for you! With a choice of over 300,000+ recipes at hand, EasyCHEF! is never short of options when providing a meal. By making EasyCHEF! simple and easy to use, even a beginner in the kitchen can feel like a master chef!
